Why Us?

The unique thing about Lahar is that we are a hub for custom knitwear. Along with basic design we specialize in gathering your requirements/preference then making those designs/outfits coming to life. We at Lahar are all about building our products to fit your needs. It's also important to note that every product coming out from our sources are all Organic cotton, Fair Trade, GOTS approved. We at Lahar want to make sure our ethical and environmental centric approach never gets hindered. Hence our motto : Style with a conscience!

Here are some of the basic options available, every product is customizable based on the buyer’s needs including labelling. Costs vary based on customizations chosen.

T-Shirts, Hoodies, Sweaters, Cardigans, scarves etc. Think anything knitwear, Think Lahar!

Types of Fabric

Poly Cotton:

As the name suggests, it is a blend of polyester and cotton fibers. The ratios can vary, usually 65% cotton and 35% polyester. The other common ratio used is 50% cotton and 50% polyester.

Benefits of Poly cotton blend:

  • Breathable.
  • Tear-resistant.
  • Reduce shrinking.
  • Can be used to create abrasion-resistant fabrics like canvas.
  • Cost-effective.

Poly-Nylon, 88%Cotton, 12% Nylon - Twill Fiber

Flame retardant, material made tougher by twilling. Protective fabrics are mostly used in work areas with hot climate. A few examples of such industries are Gas, Electric, Oil and Machinery Industries.

High Grade 97% Cotton 3% spandex:

Twill, Classic twill weave,

Features:
  • Tear-Resistant.
  • Anti-Static
  • Cotton spandex fabric.
  • Anti-wrinkle.
  • Cost-effective.

100% Combed cotton:

These are cotton fibers which are specially treated before being spun into a yarn. This increases the softness of the fibers. Improving feel and customer satisfaction in any clothing which come into direct skin contact. There are various yarn counts like 20s,30s,50s etc.

Cotton Microfiber:
  • First time cotton microfiber in 100% cotton loops.
  • Lower Cost than Terry Towels
  • Locked Loops
  • Zero-Waste
  • Washable & reusable
  • Ultra soft and durable
  • Lint and scratch free
  • Super absorbent cotton for quick drying

Pre Yarn-dyed

The yarn is dyed before making the fabric, instead of dyeing the entire fabric after it is made. This way we can avoid the ink running during the wash or before.
