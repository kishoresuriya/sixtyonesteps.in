ABOUT US

We are Lahar

Lahar’s imported textile products will not only be environmentally conscious, but also ethic-centric. We make sure all parties involved are registered with Fair-Trade, GOTS & use Organic Cotton. At Lahar we believe that fashion and ethics don’t have to be mutually exclusive, which inspired our motto “Style with a Conscience!”.

LAHAR’S VISION

Connecting cultures,

Flourishing together!

We want to connect various cultures and share their rich heritage with one another across the globe. Lahar trades and conducts business across countries all over the world. At Lahar we are set on making our benchmark as not only an ethic-centric business but also an environmentally conscious one.
